About Anubhav

Dedicated to serving communities across West Bengal

Our Mission

Based in Diamond Harbour, South 24 Parganas, West Bengal, Anubhav is committed to creating sustainable change through healthcare initiatives, educational empowerment, and environmental conservation. We focus on serving the underprivileged communities across West Bengal, with a special emphasis on rural and coastal areas.

আমাদের লক্ষ্য যে কোন ধরনের যোগাযোগের জন্য

Our Vision

To build healthier, educated, and environmentally conscious communities throughout West Bengal. We envision a state where every individual, regardless of their economic background, has access to quality healthcare, education, and lives in harmony with nature.

Our Three Core Projects

Focused initiatives creating measurable impact in West Bengal

Healthcare Initiative

Serving 2000+ Patients

Providing free medical camps, health awareness programs, and basic healthcare access to underserved communities across South 24 Parganas and neighboring districts.

  • Regular health camps
  • Free medicine distribution
  • Health awareness workshops
  • Emergency medical support
2,000+ Patients Treated
10 Health Camps

Educational Empowerment

Supporting 300+ Students

Bridging educational gaps through free tutoring, scholarship programs, and study material distribution for underprivileged children in West Bengal.

  • Free tutoring classes
  • Study material distribution
  • Career counseling
  • Merit scholarships
300+ Students Enrolled
3 Learning Centers

Environmental Conservation

Greening West Bengal

Leading environmental conservation efforts through tree plantation drives, waste management programs, and awareness campaigns in coastal areas of West Bengal.

  • Tree plantation drives
  • Coastal area cleanup
  • Plastic-free campaigns
  • Eco-awareness programs
2,700+ Beneficiaries
Multiple Green Drives
